Famous Waterfalls

Jog Falls, Karnataka

The second highest waterfall in India at 830 feet. Located on the Sharavathi River.

Nearest city: Shimoga (100 km). Train to Shimoga, then bus to Jog Falls.
Dudhsagar Falls, Goa

A four-tiered waterfall located in the Bhagwan Mahavir Wildlife Sanctuary.

Nearest station: Collem. Jeep safari from Molem. Best visited by train on the Goa-Karnataka route.
Athirappilly Falls, Kerala

The largest waterfall in Kerala, known as the "Niagara of India".

Nearest city: Chalakudy (32 km). Bus from Thrissur or Ernakulam to Athirappilly.
