Basic Tennis Rules

  • A match is played best of 3 or 5 sets.
  • A set is won by the first player to win 6 games (with a 2-game lead).
  • Scoring: Love (0), 15, 30, 40, Game.
  • A "Deuce" occurs when both players reach 40. Two consecutive points win the game.
  • The ball must land within the court boundaries. If out, the opponent wins the point.
