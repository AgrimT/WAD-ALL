Famous Landmarks

Shaniwar Wada

Shaniwar Wada
A historic fortification in the heart of Pune, built in 1732 by the Peshwa rulers.

Aga Khan Palace

Aga Khan Palace
Built in 1892, this palace is historically significant as it served as a prison for Mahatma Gandhi.

Osho Ashram

Osho Ashram
A famous meditation resort in Koregaon Park, attracting visitors from all over the world.

Sinhagad Fort

Sinhagad Fort
A historic fort located on a steep cliff, 30 km from Pune. Famous for the battle of 1670.

About Pune

Pune is a major city in Maharashtra, India. It is known for its educational institutions, IT industry, and rich Maratha heritage.
